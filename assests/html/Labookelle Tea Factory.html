Labookelle Tea Factory

Located on the right side of the main road side Labookele tea factory is easily accessible to the traveller. Visitors can enjoy a free cup of tea when visiting this factory and is always served with some kind if sweet.

Things To Do

01.Tea Tasting at the Lounge

After the tour, relax at the on-site tea lounge, where you can savor freshly brewed Ceylon tea accompanied by delightful treats like chocolate cake. The lounge offers panoramic views of the lush tea plantations, enhancing your tasting experience.

02.Explore the Tea Gardens

troll through the expansive tea gardens surrounding the factory, situated at an elevation of 5,000 feet. The serene environment and picturesque landscapes provide excellent opportunities for photography and relaxation.
