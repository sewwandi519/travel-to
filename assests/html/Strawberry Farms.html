Strawberry Farms

Strawberry Farms can be reached via Hakgala, Ambewela Road, New Zealand Farm Road. Freshly picked Strawberries are available at many outlets and cafes that serve Strawberry drinks, Ice cream pancakes etc. and very moderating priced. Grown at Ambewela Farm. Adam Agro, Rose Garden and many other farms located in Nuwara Eliya.

Things To Do

01.Strawberry Picking

Engage in the hands-on experience of picking fresh strawberries. allows visitors to wander through their fields and pick ripe strawberries during the season, typically from October to May.

02. Farm Tours

Learn about strawberry cultivation by touring the farms. Ramya Horticulture offers guided tours of their greenhouses for a nominal fee, providing insights into the farming process .
